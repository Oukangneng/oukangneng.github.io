Statistic: As of October 10, 2019, the PPD contained information on a total of 9,975 cases of political or religious imprisonment in China. Of those, 1,598 are cases of political and religious prisoners currently known or believed to be detained or imprisoned, and 8,377 are cases of prisoners who are known or believed to have been released, or executed, who died while imprisoned or soon after release, or who escaped.
